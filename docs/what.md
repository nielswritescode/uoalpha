---
title: What does UOα plan to do?
layout: default
---
- Lists Unconditional Organizing (UO) instances within the philosophy. People in agreement with the philosophy will be able to easily find UO instances that suit their needs.
- Stores research data from UO instances.
- Processes research data from UO instances by writing articles, statistical references or blogs. This output is then made publicly available and acts as support for instances. An instance can simply refer to such an article to explain people the reasoning behind their events.
- List other UOPs.
- Provide frameworks for making instances.
- Provide frameworks for making UOPs.
