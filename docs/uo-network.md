## UOα in a network of UOPs

Naturally, different UOPs might emerge. What if UOα lists all other known UOPs, especially those with a different view? Instances could then always choose which UOP to follow. An instance can be part of multilpe UOPs if they so choose. Perhaps they share parts of multiple UOPs. There will be a process of natural selection and a natural integrity check. If a UOP does not list other UOPs (is not open to other views). UOα will warn against this.
