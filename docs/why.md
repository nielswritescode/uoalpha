title: Why UOalpha?
layout: default
---

Assume there's a range between Conditional Organizing (CO) and UO. The vertical line represents where we are. The arrows represent leaps towards UO. The encircled area towards UO represents events that are highly unconditional. 
Hypothesis: These events have great potential for collective healing.
UOα is based on the belief that it is worth experimenting with a subset of these leaps because of both the potential of highly unconditional events and the healing potential of experimenting itself.
This subset can change as research data is gathered.
