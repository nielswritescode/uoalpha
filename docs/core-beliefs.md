Core beliefs are at the basis of UOalpha. 

- We are open to the possibility that these core beliefs could be wrong.
- Almost any concept is not black or white and can be seen as a range. The philosophy does classify concepts into dichotomes or categories in order to impact reality. 
- There is a huge collective trauma. 
- Rational understanding is not enough. Embodied understanding needs to be part of every decision. 
- Power should be used responsibly or not at all.
- Radical honesty/transparancy does not work. Given UOalpha's power we have a very high responsibility to be as honest and transparent as possible. 
- Humility.
- Open to not knowing.
- (Apparent) paradoxes exist and are welcome to exist. We believe in non-duality.
