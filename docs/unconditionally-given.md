This page holds guidelines for various cost types under the UOalpha philosophy. These are just guidelines, ranges that have been temporarily categorized (and can be infinitly re-categorized). Events will likely swing around these guidelines. There will need to be a form of trust building between participants and organizers.

Example of how an event could use this:

> **Mindful Soul Walk**
> Location: Amsterdam
> Cost: Unconditionally Given (UOα)

Or:

> **Somatic Touch Healing**
> Location: Breda
> Cost: Unconditionally Given under the UOalpha philosophy.

**"Unconditionally Given"**

- There is no expectation of reciprocity.
  - Events do not have an entry fee. 
  - There is no (implicit) expectation to donate.
- The event makes no explicit attempt at changing anyone's ideology or spirituality. 
- The event is not used for marketing.
  - The facilitator is welcome to mention their other (commercial) activities, as long as:
    - The event was not a 'taster' of any kind, but a full standalone experience.
    - It is explicitly mentioned that these activities are not unconditionally given. 

**"Unconditionally Given with Shared Space Costs" [UGwSSC]**

- Participants share in the cost for the space. 
- There is no expectation of reciprocity for the facilitation.
  - There is no (implicit) expectation to donate for the facilitation
- The event makes no explicit attempt at changing anyone's ideology or spirituality. 
- The event is not used for marketing.
  - The facilitator is welcome to mention their other (commercial) activities, as long as:
    - The event was not a 'taster' of any kind, but a full standalone experience.
    - It is explicitly mentioned that these activities are not unconditionally given.
